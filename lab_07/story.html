The day I first arrived in US


The day I first arrived in US is Aug.4th 2019. I arrived at 4 A.M. that morning, and took a taxi to my appartment.
The appartment is brand new, with luxury decoration. But when I enter my room, I found that there's nothing in the room. No bed, no chair, nothing!
I'm really tired so I slept on the floor for couple hours xD. That's my first in US.


After I got up, I went to a supermarket to buy some food. However, I cannot distinguish coins between 10 cent, 25 cent and 5 cent.
The employee of market helped me a lot. She told me how to distinguish these coins xD.
That's my first day in US!
